A Shaky Picture Has No Weight (2013)

Live performance including mixed-media installation with moving elements, HD video projection and sound.

Commissioned by Performa 13 and performed at Abrons Art Center, New York

Total running time: 40 minutes.

Performers: Vishal Jugdeo and Billy Wright

Technical direction and lighting design: Kryssy Wright

Theater tecnicians: Chimmy Gunn, Dan Cruz, Ben Demarest, McKenzie Angelo, Yolanda Royster

Production coordinator: Robert Hickerson

Abrons technical director: Jon Harper

Crew in Los Angeles: director of photography: Conci Althouse, sound mix: Noah Liebelt, production assistant: Eric Sick. Script, direction and editing: Vishal Jugdeo. Camera in Guyana: Vishal Jugdeo.
